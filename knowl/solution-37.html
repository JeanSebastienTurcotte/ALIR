Solution 1.2.4.7.a.1.
\begin{align*} \norm{\vec{u}+\vec{v}}^2+\norm{\vec{u}-\vec{v}}^2 &=(\vec{u}+\vec{v})\cdot(\vec{u}+\vec{v})+(\vec{u}-\vec{v})\cdot(\vec{u}-\vec{v}) &\text{ (par 4.)}\\ &= \vec{u}\cdot\vec{u}+2\vec{u}\cdot\vec{v}+\vec{v}\cdot\vec{v}+\vec{u}\cdot\vec{u}-2\vec{u}\cdot\vec{v}+\vec{v}\cdot\vec{v} &\text{ (par 1. et 3.)}\\ &= 2(\vec{u}\cdot\vec{u})+2(\vec{v}\cdot\vec{v})&\\ &= 2\norm{\vec{u}}^2+2\norm{\vec{v}}^2 &\text{ (par 4.)} \end{align*}
