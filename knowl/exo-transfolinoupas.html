(a)
\(T_1\begin{pmatrix}x\\y\end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix}x+1\\y-1\end{pmatrix}\)
Réponse.Solution.
(b)
\(T_2\begin{pmatrix}x\\y\end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix}x^2\\-y\end{pmatrix}\)
Réponse.Solution.
(c)
\(T_3\begin{pmatrix}x\\y\end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix}2x-y\\x\end{pmatrix}\)
Réponse.Solution.
(d)
\(T_4\begin{pmatrix}x\\y\end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix}1/x\\y\end{pmatrix}\)
Réponse.Solution.
(e)
\(T_5\begin{pmatrix}x\\y\end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix}x+3y\\0\end{pmatrix}\)
Réponse.Solution.
(f)
\(T_6\begin{pmatrix}x\\y\end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix}y\\x\end{pmatrix}\)
Réponse.Solution.
