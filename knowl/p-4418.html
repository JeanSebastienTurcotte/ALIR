On exprime \(\vec{u}\) dans la base \(\mathcal{B}=\langle \vec{v}_1,\vec{v}_2\rangle\text{.}\)
\begin{align*} \vec{u}&=k_1\vec{v}_1+k_2\vec{v}_2\\ (19,17)&=k_1(7,3)+k_2(-8,1)\\ \Rightarrow &\begin{cases}19&=7k_1-8k_2\\ 17&=3k_1+k_2\end{cases}\\ \Rightarrow &\begin{cases}k_1&=5\\ k_2&=2\end{cases} \end{align*}
Donc, on obtient
\begin{equation*} \vec{u}=5\vec{v}_1+2\vec{v}_2=(5,2)_\mathcal{B}\text{.} \end{equation*}
